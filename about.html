About Scales Elder Law

At Scales Elder Law, we understand that navigating the complexities of elder law and estate planning can be overwhelming. Serving clients statewide, our experienced legal team is dedicated to providing compassionate, personalized guidance to protect your assets, secure your future, and ensure peace of mind for you and your loved ones.

With a focus on Medicaid planning, nursing home abuse cases, wills and trusts, and probate matters, we tailor our strategies to meet your unique needs. Whether you are preparing for long-term care, safeguarding your inheritance, or seeking justice for elder abuse, Scales Elder Law is committed to advocating fiercely on your behalf.

  • Comprehensive Planning: From Medicaid qualification to estate preservation, we develop thorough plans that protect your assets and your family’s legacy.
  • Compassionate Support: We recognize the emotional challenges involved and provide sensitive, respectful service every step of the way.
  • Statewide Reach: Whether you need local assistance or representation across the state, our firm is ready to serve you wherever you are.
