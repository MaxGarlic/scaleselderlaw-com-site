Primary Practice Area: Elder Law & Estate Planning

At Scales Elder Law, we understand that planning for the future and protecting your loved ones is a deeply personal and critical task. Our dedicated team specializes in Elder Law and Estate Planning, serving clients across the state with compassion, expertise, and unwavering commitment. Whether you are seeking Medicaid planning, fighting against nursing home abuse, establishing wills and trusts, or navigating the complexities of probate, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

Medicaid Planning

Medicaid rules can be overwhelming and constantly changing. Our attorneys provide strategic Medicaid planning to help you preserve your assets while qualifying for vital long-term care benefits. We work closely with you to create a tailored plan that maximizes your financial security and peace of mind.

Nursing Home Abuse

If you suspect a loved one is suffering from nursing home abuse or neglect, time is of the essence. Our experienced legal team aggressively advocates for victims’ rights, holding negligent facilities accountable and ensuring justice is served. We fight to protect the dignity and well-being of your family members.

Wills and Trusts

Creating a comprehensive will or trust is essential to safeguarding your estate and ensuring your wishes are honored. We help you design personalized estate plans that minimize taxes, avoid probate delays, and protect your assets for future generations.

Probate

Probate can be a complicated and stressful process. Our skilled probate attorneys streamline administration, resolve disputes, and help executors fulfill their duties efficiently. We strive to make the probate experience as smooth and straightforward as possible for your family.
