Secondary Practice Areas: Comprehensive Elder Law & Estate Planning Services

At Scales Elder Law, we understand that elder law and estate planning encompass a broad range of legal challenges. While our primary focus is on Medicaid planning, nursing home abuse, wills and trusts, and probate, our expertise extends to several critical secondary practice areas designed to protect your family’s future and ensure peace of mind.

Guardianship and Conservatorship

When a loved one becomes unable to make decisions due to aging or illness, establishing guardianship or conservatorship is essential. Our attorneys guide you through this sensitive process with compassion and precision, helping you secure the legal authority needed to manage healthcare, finances, and personal affairs responsibly.

Long-Term Care Planning

Effective long-term care planning is vital to avoid exhausting life savings while securing quality care. We provide tailored strategies that align with your financial goals, including asset protection and eligibility qualification for government benefits, to ensure your dignity and comfort throughout your senior years.

Special Needs Planning

If you have a family member with special needs, our firm helps you create customized special needs trusts and plans that safeguard their financial future without jeopardizing eligibility for public assistance. We focus on building a secure foundation that supports independence and well-being.

  • Asset Protection: Shield your savings from unnecessary risks and preserve wealth for future generations.
  • Power of Attorney: Establish durable powers of attorney for healthcare and finances to ensure your wishes are respected.
  • Advance Healthcare Directives: Prepare living wills and directives to guide medical care decisions when you can’t speak for yourself.

Our experienced team at Scales Elder Law is dedicated to delivering personalized legal solutions that address your unique circumstances. Whether you need assistance beyond our core areas or want a comprehensive plan incorporating multiple elements, we are here to provide clear guidance and aggressive advocacy.
